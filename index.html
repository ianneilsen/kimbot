About Me

I am an Accredited Mental Health Social Worker (AMHSW) committed to helping you navigate life's challenges and achieve your wellbeing goals. With a client-centered approach and evidence-based practices, I provide professional support tailored to your unique needs.

My role is to support individuals and families through mental health challenges, life transitions, and personal growth. I believe in creating a safe, non-judgmental space where healing and transformation can occur.

When you're ready to take the next step in your journey, I'm here to support you every way I can.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral?

No referral needed to attend. However, a GP Mental Health Care Plan allows you to claim Medicare rebates (up to 10 sessions per year).

How does billing work?

Pay full session fee on appointment day. Medicare rebate processes to your account within 24-48 hours.

What's the cancellation policy?

  • 72+ hours notice: No fee
  • Less than 72 hours: 50% of session fee
  • Same-day or no-show: Full session fee

What if my psychologist isn't a good fit?

Let our team know and we'll connect you with another psychologist who's a better match.

Do you accept insurance & NDIS?

Yes, we accept private health insurance, NDIS (self-managed & plan-managed), WorkCover, DVA, and EAP referrals.
