About Me

My name is Kim and I am an accredited mental health social worker, qualified to provide a range of focussed psychological strategies (FPS) and evidenced based, therapeutic modalities to support your wellbeing, recovery and healing. I have worked in a range of clinical and non-clinical settings for over 15 years and I am passionate about providing trauma-informed and client-centred care.

Having worked extensively with those healing from gender-based violence, I value trust and safety, and can support you through a range of challenges including the impacts of trauma, mental health challenges, relationship difficulties, life transitions & adjustment and work-related stress including vicarious trauma, burnout and moral injury. I also provide clinical supervision to social workers and other allied health professionals.

My foundational approach is Schema Therapy (ST) but I also incorporate Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT), Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) into my practice depending on your needs. Most importantly, I work with you to determine what you want to get out of counselling and together develop realistic, sustainable recovery goals and treatment plans.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral?

Self referral's accepted. However, a GP Mental Health Care Plan allows you to claim Medicare rebates (up to 10 sessions per year).

How does billing work?

Pay full session fee on appointment day. Medicare rebate processes to your account within 24-48 hours.

What's the cancellation policy?

Notice period for cancellation or to re-schedule is a minimum of 24 hours, no penalty incurred.

Late cancellation (less than 24 hours) and no shows, will incur a fee of 50% of session price.
